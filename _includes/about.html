About Entangled

Do you remember the last time that you were alone? How did that feel? Have you ever stood in a crowded room and felt completely alone. That describes some of you.

Entangled was born out of a desire to solve the problem of loneliness. It’s the intersection of so many ideas and feelings. The deep and instant connection I felt meeting my girlfriend for the first time, the big hole in my heart for the sudden death of my father, and my interest in Quantum Entanglement.

You will be entangled. Where ever you go, they will go. Once you’re entangled, you will never be untangled again.
